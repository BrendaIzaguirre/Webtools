What is Betty White known for?
Betty White, in full Betty Marion White, American actress best known for her comedic work on numerous television sitcoms, most notably The Mary Tyler Moore Show and The Golden Girls. White grew up in Los Angeles.

How did Betty White impact the world?
In 1954, she took a public stance against racism when she went against complaints about having a Black performer on her TV show. She continued to take a stand as she fought for LGBTQIA+ rights over the years.

Why was Betty White so amazing?
Because She is Talented - She stole everyone's heart while starring in 'Golden Girls' for seven years. She's also starred in other notable shows and movies, including 'The Mary Tyler Moore Show,' 'Hot In Cleveland,' 'Bones,' 'The Bold and the Beautiful' and '30 Rock.

If she hadn't been an actor, White would have been a zookeeper.
"Hands down," she confessed in a 2014 interview. This should come as little surprise to those aware of White's reputation as an avid animal lover and activist. Not only did she try to visit the local zoo of wherever she traveled, but she was also a supporter of the Farm Animal Reform Movement and Friends of Animals group, as well as a Los Angeles Zoo board member, who donated "tens of thousands of dollars" over the past several decades. In 2010, White founded a T-shirt line whose profits go to the Morris Animal Foundation, one of her most beloved charities.

Oldest person to earn an Emmy nomination?
White earned an Emmy nod for Outstanding Host for a Reality or Reality-Competition Program for the senior citizen-centric prank show Betty White's Off Their Rockers. She was 92.
